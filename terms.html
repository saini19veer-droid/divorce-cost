5. Accuracy of Information

DivorceCostBreakdown.com makes reasonable efforts to keep cost estimates and legal information current and accurate. However, the following factors mean estimates will vary from actual costs:

  • Attorney hourly rates vary widely even within the same city or state
  • Court filing fees change periodically and vary by county within states
  • Case complexity is difficult to predict — what begins as uncontested can become contested
  • Mediation costs depend on the mediator's rate and number of sessions required
  • Asset division, alimony, and child support calculations depend on specific state formulas and judicial discretion

Always verify current court fees directly with your local courthouse and obtain written fee agreements from any attorney you retain.
