Divorce Costs in Wyoming: The Full Breakdown

The cost of divorce in Wyoming ranges from roughly $2,100 for a simple uncontested case to $24,000 or more for a contested divorce that goes to trial. The single biggest factor is not the state — it is how much you and your spouse disagree.

Wyoming has the lowest divorce filing fees in the US (tied with Mississippi at $70) and a 20-day waiting period.

How to Reduce Your Divorce Costs in Wyoming

  • Reach agreement before engaging attorneys. The difference in Wyoming between arriving with a rough agreement and arriving with nothing is typically $4,600–$11,500 in fees.
  • Consider mediation. Most mediations resolve in 3–6 sessions ($2,000–$6,000 total) versus $10,000–$40,000 for litigation.
